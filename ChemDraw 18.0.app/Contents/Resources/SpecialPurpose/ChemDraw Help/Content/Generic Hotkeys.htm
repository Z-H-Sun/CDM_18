Generic Hotkeys (tool switching hotkeys)

In ChemDraw 18.0, generic hotkeys have been introduced to eliminate the need of having to modify the hotkeys.xml file, as was the case in all prior versions. When the mouse is not over an atom or the bond, you can press a key on the keyboard to switch between tools. The key to press is indicated between brackets in the tooltip when you mouse over the toolbar. The generic hotkeys are case sensitive, for example, pressing [x] will select the single bond tool, while pressing [Shift+x] will select the acyclic chain tool.
