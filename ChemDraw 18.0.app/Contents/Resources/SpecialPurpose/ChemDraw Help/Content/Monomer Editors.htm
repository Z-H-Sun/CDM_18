Monomer Editors

In ChemDraw 18.0 three types of Monomer Viewers or Editors are available:
Monomer Viewer
Monomer Editor
Document Monomer Importer
You can access the monomer editors from the HELM Monomers toolbar. Depending on the type of data source (Read-Only, Writable or Document) you selected, the respective viewer or editor is displayed when you click on the Open Monomer Editor button.
